Jennifer Crow

Grief & Trauma Response Consultant

- Based in Portland, Oregon -

Jennifer Crow specializes in developing immediate strategies for businesses that have been impacted by a tragedy. She offers:

  • On-site assessments
  • Phone consultations
  • Customized plans for how to respond effectively as a business
  • Strategies and approaches to best support employees impacted
  • Educational resources
  • The development of both short term and long term assistance strategies
  • Management and human resource department advising
  • Communication response methods
  • In-person and phone support to grieving employees
  • Approaches specific to sudden death, suicide, terminal illness diagnosis

- Background -

For the past 10 years, Jennifer Crow has been dedicated to supporting and educating those who experience grief. As an active member of the community, Jennifer has been involved in debriefings, facilitated meetings, and has supported those affected by sudden lossâ€”in hospitals, businesses, community centers, and schools. She is a hospice volunteer, an end-of-life companion, and has worked with local organizations to support those grieving the loss of loved ones. She studied in Dublin, Ireland with the Irish Hospice Foundation, completing their Training and Education series on Loss & Bereavement. She is a member of ADEC, the Association for Death Education and Counseling.

Jennifer has been trained in many aspects of bereavement, including trauma response, complicated grief support, anticipatory and unexpected loss, and disenfranchised grief. She is a strong advocate for grief education and shifting society towards a place of better support for those grieving loss. Jennifer is currently a mentor with the University of Portland class Theological Dimensions of Suffering and Death. She has experience with victim response at the scenes of tragedies, and assists businesses in supporting their grieving employees in the wake of a tragedy.
